# Business Model

TerraSense employs a multi-tiered business model designed to balance rapid farmer adoption with sustainable revenue generation through B2B partnerships and data monetization.

## 1. Direct-to-Farmer (B2C)
* **Hardware Sales**: Subsidized or at-cost edge nodes.
* **Subscription**: A freemium model where basic telemetry is free, but advanced AI insights (disease prediction, precision fertilizer application) require a low-cost seasonal subscription.
* **Voice-as-a-Service**: Premium vernacular voice support for hands-free, accessible farming advice.

## 2. Enterprise & Agribusiness (B2B)
* **Data as a Service (DaaS)**: Aggregated, anonymized micro-climate and soil health data sold to agricultural research firms, seed manufacturers, and insurance companies.
* **Supply Chain Integration**: Providing yield predictions to logistics and FMCG companies to optimize their procurement supply chains.

## 3. Government & NGO Partnerships (B2G)
* **Subsidies**: Partnering with agricultural ministries to deploy nodes at scale.
* **Climate Resilience**: Providing macro-level data for climate change adaptation strategies and resource allocation.
